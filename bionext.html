    why join BioNext

    Not just a conference

    Join us for the opening track of the event, known as BioNext, a dynamic one-day event hosted by Taranto Biotech Days on October 11. Set against the picturesque backdrop of the city of Taranto, Italy, this gathering promises an inspiring convergence of minds of the Biotech Industry. Whether you're a seasoned professional or a curious newcomer, BioNext offers a unique opportunity to explore the latest advancements, network with Industry Leaders, and engage in thought-provoking discussions. Don't miss out on this chance to be at the forefront of Biotech Innovation in a vibrant and historic setting.

    Personal Development

    Acquire new skills, perspectives, and insights to foster both personal and professional growth.

    Data Science, Computational Models and related Technologies

    @ Massimo Chiriatti, Lenovo
    @ Simone Bianco, Altos Lab
    @ Pietro Liò, Cambridge University

    How will we manage the transformative impact of AI and Big Data in scientific research? The panel will explore the use of big data and AI in scientific research and the importance of data collection and management in predictive and precision medicine and discuss how large datasets and advanced AI algorithms are revolutionizing the way we understand, diagnose, and treat diseases. Emphasis will be placed on the significance of accurate and comprehensive data collection, as well as effective data management practices, in enabling the development of personalized treatment plans and predictive models. The session will also highlight real-world applications and case studies showcasing the potential of big data and AI to enhance patient outcomes, drive innovation, and pave the way for a new era of precision healthcare.
