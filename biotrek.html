    BioTrek

    Join us as we unveil the hidden gems of Taranto.

    Join us for the second event of the track, on October 12. Set in the stunning city of Taranto, this event offers a unique exploration of the city's natural beauties and a deep dive into its rich history. Embark on an enchanting journey that combines the allure of marine life with the intrigue of ancient history. End your day with an exceptional networking dinner in a historic setting, savoring the flavors of Apulia. Don’t miss out on the chance to connect with nature, history, and like-minded individuals in a truly memorable experience.
